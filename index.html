Adventures of a hospital Pharmacy Technician

What does a Pharmacy Technician do exactly?

  • Compound IV medications
  • Fill Pyxis medstations
  • Manage inventory of medications
  • Clean and maintain pharmacy equipment/clean rooms
  • Mixing and dispensing oral medications
  • Unit-dosing tablets

Sterile IV compounding

IV compounding is the practice of mixing intravenous medications in a sterile environment such as a laminar flow hood/isolator/or biological safety cabinet.

Filling Pyxis medstations

Pyxis is an automated medication dispensing system that allows nurses easy access to patients medications

Managing inventory

The hospital pharmacy carries many medications: Oral/topical/IV/inhalation/etc. It is the responsibility of technicians to ensure that medication are instock and reordered when necessary.

More duties

Technicians also mix oral medications such as Amoxicillin. All pills dispensed from the hospital pharmacy must be unit-dosed (or individually packaged).
